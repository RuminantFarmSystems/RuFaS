Anaerobic Digestion Discussion
==============================
**Manure Module discussion 6/6/2022**

**Housing Type**

| **System Bedding options:**
| -Might be good to specify the type of organics available since the
  nutrient
| composition/degradability etc will vary depending on the type selected
| -Manure separated solids, straw, recycled paper, wood shavings/sawdust
| -geotextile (synthetic) bedding (which may be used in addition with an
  organic layer, just less of it)
| **Housing type:**
| -holding area could be feeding area/vs bedding area

| **System options:**
| -there is also the deep pack type which is irregularly cleaned out, so
  might want to include the frequency of cleaning whether scrape or
  flush or deep pack.

| **Processing:**
| **System options:**
| -Maybe have a “no treatment” Ie straight to lagoon option
| -Have ability to select multiple of these treatments, and the order in
  which they occur (ie anaerobic digestion to solid/liquid separation to
  lagoon or solid/liquid separation to AD to lagoon, etc).

| **Storage:**
| **System options:**
| -for storage options should have the ability to select whether a
  lagoon is covered or not. Also if it’s covered if there is a flare in
  place to combust produced biogas
| -uncovered storage will also have to consider the addition of
  rainwater loss to evaporation, to the total volumes to be handled.

   -may want to include the frequency of emptying of storages

| -may want to include if the storages are emptied by truck, or if there
  is a pipeline or other distribution system in place
| -Should also include the construction type of the storage system.
  Whether it is lined or unlined (which will affect losses from the
  lagoon.) ie clay vs synthetic liners
| -Volatile solids loading rate of lagoon will affect not only methane
  emissions, but odor (could be a very important consideration)
| -Should be able to set the volume of the lagoon which also includes
  depth of the lagoon -is water from the lagoon recycled to use as flush
  water
| -frequency of sludge cleanout (typically 10 years), but could be
  different.

   -agitation used (if a non-sludge design).

   -type of bedding and cleaning will often affect the design of a
   storage system

====================================
**Example ration based on NRC 2001**
====================================

A linear programming with rumen degradable protein limiting microbial
crude protein production (LP_RDPlim), a linear programing with total
digestible nutrient limiting microbial crude protein production
(LP_TDNlim) and a nonlinear programming (NLP) were developed based on
Nutrient Requirements for Dairy Cattle (2001). Two sets of feed were
used to formulate diets for three animal groups (lactating cows, dry
cows and heifers). Animal information and sample diets are shown below.

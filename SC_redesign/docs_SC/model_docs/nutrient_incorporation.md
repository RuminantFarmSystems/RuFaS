### __Nutrient incorporation__

*Under construction* 

The module currently holds two classes (`NitrogenIncorporation` and `PhosphorusIncorporation`) to represent nitrogen and phosphorus uptake and incorporation into 
plant biomass. However, since both have identical methods (excluding nitrogen fixation), they will soon be combined into a single class (`NutrientIncorporation`) for 
simplicity.

---
**References**: this module is based upon the "Nutrient Uptake by Plants" section of the [SWAT][1] model (5:2.3).

[1]:https://swat.tamu.edu/media/99192/swat2009-theory.pdf
